## 🎨 Master Visual Style Guide (Option A: Shinkai Anime Dreamscape)

Every shot in this production is locked to a high-fidelity **Modern Anime (Makoto Shinkai / CoMix Wave Films style)** aesthetic. 

### 1. Style Key Elements
* **Backgrounds:** Hyper-detailed, painterly watercolor environments. Wheat field with detailed individual stalks, golden-orange husks catching twilight rays.
* **Atmosphere:** Ethereal volumetric light shafts (crepuscular rays/chihaya-kaze), floating glowing dust particles, soft lens bloom, subtle chromatic aberration at the lens edges, high-contrast twilight sky with saturated, sweeping cumulus clouds.
* **Master Hex Palette:**
  * **Golden Hour / Hope:** `#FFD700` (Golden Yellow), `#FF8C00` (Dark Orange), `#FFD700` (Wheat Glow)
  * **Twilight Sky:** `#191970` (Midnight Blue), `#4B0082` (Indigo), `#E6E6FA` (Lavender bloom)
  * **Auras:** `#1E90FF` (Steel Blue), `#E32636` (Crimson), `#00FA9A` (Turquoise-Green)

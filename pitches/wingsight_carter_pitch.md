## 1. The Strategic Opportunity
Wing Sight has established itself as the premier bridge for Chinese cinema at Cannes. With the launch of AIGC titles like *The Reunion Journey* and *Legends of the South*, Wing Sight has proven the potential of AI-generated content. 

However, to truly establish Chinese AIGC as a global industry standard, we must move beyond festival showcases and enter **commercial theatrical distribution, TV broadcasting, and Oscar validation.**

We propose a strategic alliance between **Wing Sight**, **our AI Film Platform**, and **Carter Pilcher (Founder & CEO of Shorts International & ShortsTV)**—the world’s leading short film distributor and exclusive presenter of the Oscar Nominated Short Films.

## 2. Why Wing Sight Needs This Alliance

By partnering with Carter Pilcher through our platform, Wing Sight unlocks immediate, global distribution assets that are otherwise inaccessible:

```
[ Chinese AIGC Shorts ] ──► [ ShortsTV Network ] ──► 100M+ Households Globally
[ (Co-produced by WS) ] ──► [ Theatrical Release ] ──► Oscar Qualification & Box Office
```

1. **The Oscar Pathway**: Carter Pilcher’s company has held the exclusive theatrical release rights for the Oscar Nominated Short Films since 2006. This partnership provides a direct, structured pipeline to get Chinese AIGC short films qualified for Academy Award consideration.
2. **Massive Global Reach**: ShortsTV broadcasts to **100 million households** across the US, Europe, Latin America, and South Asia. This provides immediate commercial TV distribution for co-produced content.
3. **Cannes & BAFTA Alignment**: Carter Pilcher is a voting member of both **AMPAS (the Oscars Academy)** and **BAFTA**. Aligning with him elevates the artistic prestige of Wing Sight’s projects, disarming Western critics who are skeptical of AI filmmaking.

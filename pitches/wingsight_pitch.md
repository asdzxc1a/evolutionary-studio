## 1. Executive Summary
Following Wing Sight’s historic showcases at the 79th Cannes Film Festival (including *The Reunion Journey* and *Legends of the South*), it is clear that AI-generated content (AIGC) is transitioning from experimental short films to full-length commercial releases. However, the core challenges remain: **maintaining visual consistency, lowering computational barriers for indie creators, and matching AI-generated visual drafts with high-end European post-production and distribution networks.**

We propose a strategic partnership between **Wing Sight** and our upcoming **AI Film Platform**. Our platform provides young directors with specialized, production-grade tools powered by the best underlying Chinese foundation models (Kling AI, Vidu). By combining our technology and director pool with Wing Sight’s deep European distribution network and creative professionals, we can co-produce the next wave of award-winning AIGC films.

## 2. The Collaboration Model: "Tech + Art + Distribution"

We propose a three-part ecosystem:

```
[ Young Directors / Artists ] 
           │ (Visual Vision & Direction)
           ▼
  [ Our AI Platform ] ◄── (Unified access to Kling/Vidu + Visual Consistency Tools)
           │ (High-fidelity AIGC Drafts)
           ▼
[ European Post-Production ] ◄── (Wing Sight's network of editors, sound designers, colorists)
           │ (Theater-Ready Cinematic Cut)
           ▼
[ International Distribution ] ◄── (Wing Sight's European distribution partners & Cannes Showcase)
```

1. **The Technology (Our Platform)**: We provide a unified studio environment. Creators use advanced camera controls, consistency engines, and multi-shot timelines.
2. **The Professionals (Wing Sight's European Network)**: Wing Sight introduces European producers, editors, and sound engineers to collaborate with the directors. They polish the raw AI video output, adding professional scoring, color grading, and voice/audio syncing.
3. **The Distribution (Wing Sight)**: The resulting co-productions are distributed globally, leveraging Wing Sight's copyright expertise and partnerships in Europe, culminating in a dedicated showcase at the **Cannes 2027 Marché du Film / China Night**.

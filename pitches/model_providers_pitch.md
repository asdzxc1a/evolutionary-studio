## 1. Context & Opportunity
Chinese video foundation models—specifically **Kling AI** and **Vidu**—have reached parity with, or exceeded, Western counterparts (Sora, Runway Gen-3) in motion dynamics, physical accuracy, and camera control. However, these models are primarily consumed as consumer web apps, generating short-form viral clips. 

To transition from "viral clip generators" to **industrial filmmaking tools**, these models need to be embedded in professional director workflows. 

Our platform is building an **AIGC Director Studio** specifically designed for young filmmakers. We propose a strategic partnership where we integrate your API models directly into our visual editing suite, driving high-volume enterprise API usage and producing high-visibility cinematic showcases on the international stage (e.g., Cannes, Venice).

---

## 2. Partnership Structure

### What We Offer You:
1. **Enterprise API Consumption**: We route thousands of young filmmakers' rendering requests through your enterprise API endpoints, generating consistent, paid computational volume.
2. **Professional Feature Showcases**: We are partnering with **Beijing Wing Sight Culture & Media** (organizers of Cannes "China Night") to distribute AIGC movies made on our platform. Your model will receive credit as the **Core AI Visual Engine** in film credits, press releases, and festival screens.
3. **Structured Creative Feedback & Data**: We will capture professional user interactions (e.g., prompting sequences, camera adjustments, editing timeline cuts) and feed back anonymized telemetry to help your teams refine model weights, prompt adherence, and physics controls.

### What We Request from You:
1. **High-Throughput, Low-Latency API Access**: Dedicated rate limits on your text-to-video, image-to-video, and professional camera control endpoints.
2. **Developer Discount / Compute Credits**: A discounted enterprise pricing tier during our launch phase, or co-marketing compute credits to distribute to selected directors.
3. **Early Access to Beta Features**: Early access to new releases (e.g., character consistency updates, prompt conditioning, localized editing brushes) so we can build integrations before they go public.

```
[ User UI: Editor Timeline ]

            ▼

            └──────► Shengshu Vidu API (For physics-intense environment & cinematic scenes)
```

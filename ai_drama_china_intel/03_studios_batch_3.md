### Flagship Production: 《三星堆·未来启示录》(Sanxingdui: Future Apocalypse)

- **Format**: 12–13 episode sci-fi short drama series
- **Release**: July 8, 2024 on Douyin
- **Views**: 135M on Douyin; 140M+ total cross-platform (as of Aug 2024); near 200M cumulative
- **Distinction**: China's first fully AIGC continuous-narrative sci-fi short drama series
- **Partners**: Co-produced by Douyin; 即梦AI (Jimeng AI) as chief technology provider
- **Film sequel**: 《三星堆：未来往事》received public screening license (龙标) in 2026 — China's first AI-deeply-involved theatrical film with cinema release

### 7-Layer Pipeline

#### LAYER 1: SCRIPT (剧本)
- Based on Bona's 2022 film IP for the Sanxingdui franchise
- LLM-assisted script development with historical experts and sci-fi writers for domain guidance
- Human-led creative decisions; AI assists with text generation and worldbuilding
- Operating mode: "边研究、边开发、边创作" (research, develop, create simultaneously)
- No mature industrial-grade AI filmmaking software existed — the team built processes from scratch

#### LAYER 2: STORYBOARD / SHOT PLANNING (分镜)
- Full storyboard design phase integrated into the pipeline
- Visual pre-visualization using AI-generated concept art
- Manual decomposition of 12 episodes into shot sequences with AI-assisted previsualization
- Comprehensive world-building for ancient Sanxingdui civilization with sci-fi overlays
- AI excels at large-scale environment construction and spectacle scenes

#### LAYER 3: VISUAL GENERATION (画面生成)
- **Primary tool**: 即梦AI (Jimeng AI) — Douyin's generative AI platform
- Fused 10+ cutting-edge AI technologies including diffusion models for shot generation
- Text-to-video and image-to-video generation workflows
- Camera movement control and frame editing capabilities
- **Core challenge**: Character emotional consistency, limb coordination, physical logic, and scene-to-scene continuity
- Significant human intervention required to compensate for model "blind spots" in complex logic
- Team developed specialized film-vertical large models and professional film knowledge bases

#### LAYER 4: AUDIO / VOICE (音频/配音)
- Advanced TTS (Text-to-Speech) synthesis for character dialogue
- AI-generated background music to construct the sci-fi soundscape
- Full audio pipeline: voice synthesis + music generation AI + sound effects

#### LAYER 5: EDITING / POST-PRODUCTION (后期制作)
- Ultra-HD rendering and frame interpolation (补帧)
- Extensive human post-production optimization on AI-generated base materials
- Traditional film editing sensibilities applied to AI-generated footage
- The "easiest parts of live-action" (casual dialogue, subtle expressions) became the hardest for AI

#### LAYER 6: QUALITY CONTROL (质控)
- Historical experts consulted for content accuracy (Sanxingdui archaeology)
- Sci-fi writers involved for narrative coherence
- Heavy human QC layer to address AI inconsistencies in emotion, physics, and continuity
- Iterative feedback loops between AI generation and human review

#### LAYER 7: DISTRIBUTION / MONETIZATION (分发/变现)
- **Primary platform**: Douyin (exclusive initial release)
- Reached top 5 on Douyin's short drama trending chart
- Cross-platform propagation exceeding 140M views
- Strategic value: market validation for future AI-cinema projects rather than direct monetization focus
- Film version (《未来往事》) represents upgrade to theatrical distribution

### Production Metrics
- **Team model**: In-house R&D team + film industrialization production process
- **Production mode**: "Human-machine co-creation" — not pure automation
- **Cost reduction**: AI significantly reduces costs for complex VFX and environment creation
- **Competitive advantage**: Film-studio pedigree applied to AI content; IP pipeline from short drama → theatrical film

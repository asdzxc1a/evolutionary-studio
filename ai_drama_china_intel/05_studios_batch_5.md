## Case Study A: 《万兽独尊》(Beast Lord) — THE FLAGSHIP CASE

> **This is the most important case study — the proof that AI drama Agent pipelines work at scale.**

| Field | Detail |
|:------|:-------|
| **Producer** | 创翊传媒 (Chuangyi Media) |
| **Tool** | ByteDance Xiaoyunque Agent + Seedance 2.0 |
| **Team** | 5 people |
| **Timeline** | ~8 days |
| **Output** | 60 episodes (~40,000+ character script) |
| **Views** | 100M+ on Douyin in 4 days |
| **Chart** | #1 on Hongguo Manga Drama hot chart |

### EXACT Step-by-Step Workflow

| Step | Action | Tool/System | Responsible |
|:-----|:-------|:------------|:------------|
| 1 | Upload full novel/script (up to 100K chars) | Xiaoyunque Drama Agent | Script Editor |
| 2 | AI deep-parses worldview, timeline, character arcs, plot logic | Seedance 2.0 backend | Automated |
| 3 | Character lifecycle management: lock faces, costumes, aging across all episodes | Xiaoyunque character system | Creative Director reviews |
| 4 | Auto-generate story blueprint + scene designs | Xiaoyunque Agent | Automated |
| 5 | Auto-storyboard: shot breakdown with camera angles (wide/medium/close/extreme) | Xiaoyunque Agent | Director reviews & adjusts |
| 6 | Batch video generation in chosen style (2D/3D/photorealistic) | Seedance 2.0 | Automated + manual curation |
| 7 | Custom edits: fine-tune storyboards, character images, camera movements | Xiaoyunque editor interface | Creative team |
| 8 | Audio: AI dubbing with character-specific voices, BGM, SFX | Platform audio tools | Audio Editor |
| 9 | Final assembly, rhythm check, quality calibration | Xiaoyunque + manual review | Team Lead |
| 10 | Multi-platform export + publish | Platform export tools | Team Lead |

### Key Technical Breakthroughs

1. **Story Comprehension**: Seedance 2.0 doesn't just generate images — it "understands" the story structure, enabling logically coherent serialized production
2. **Character Consistency at Scale**: Full-lifecycle tracking prevents the #1 AI drama problem (characters changing appearance between shots/episodes)
3. **Multi-modal Reference**: Supports simultaneous input of reference images, videos, audio, and text for precise character reproduction
4. **Template Accumulation**: Best parameter configurations saved as reusable templates → cross-episode, cross-project consistency
5. **"One-Click to Film" with Override**: Automation for speed, manual controls for quality — the critical balance

### Why It Went Viral

- **Genre match**: "Beast Lord" = power fantasy/xuanhuan → high engagement genre for manga drama audience
- **Volume**: 60 episodes = massive content library for algorithmic distribution
- **Quality floor**: Seedance 2.0's character consistency eliminated the "uncanny" feeling that kills most AI dramas
- **Speed-to-market**: 8-day turnaround means rapid trend capture

## Case Study B: 《三星堆：未来启示录》(Sanxingdui: Future Apocalypse)

| Field | Detail |
|:------|:-------|
| **Producer** | 博纳影业 AIGMS Center (Bona Film Group) |
| **Co-producer** | Douyin (TikTok China) |
| **Chief Tech** | 即梦AI (Jimeng AI / Dreamina) |
| **Format** | Season 1: 13 episodes, 3-4 min each (planned: 2 seasons × 12 eps = 24 total) |
| **Views** | ~200M+ cumulative |
| **Historic First** | First AIGC drama to receive 《网络剧片发行许可证》(Network Drama Distribution License) |
| **Launch** | July 8, 2024 |

### How a Major Studio Organized AI Production

**Core Approach: "AI+" not "Film+AI"**
- AI is the core creative partner, not a post-production add-on
- Applied 10+ AI technologies across the entire pipeline

### Technology Stack

| Category | Technologies Used |
|:---------|:-----------------|
| **Scriptwriting** | Large Language Models (LLM) |
| **Visual Concept/Storyboard** | Text-to-image generation, Diffusion Models, GANs, Style Transfer |
| **Character Generation** | AI character creation (e.g., character "Wu Xingyan") |
| **Video Production** | Image-to-video conversion |
| **Post-production** | Super-resolution upscaling, Text-to-Speech (TTS), AI music generation, auto-editing algorithms |
| **Enhancement** | Media content enhancement tools |

### Production Workflow

1. **World-building**: Define Sanxingdui sci-fi universe, mythology framework, visual tone
2. **Script Generation**: LLM-assisted scriptwriting with human creative direction
3. **Concept & Storyboard**: Diffusion models + GANs generate concept art and scene storyboards
4. **Character Design**: AI generates character appearances with style transfer for consistency
5. **Image-to-Video**: Convert approved storyboard frames into video sequences
6. **Video Editing**: AI-assisted editing with narrative pacing optimization
7. **Audio Layer**: TTS for dialogue, AI-generated music score, sound design
8. **Enhancement**: Super-resolution upscaling for broadcast quality
9. **Human Curation**: Team selects, refines, and adjusts from multiple AI-generated options throughout

### Key Challenges Documented

- **No integrated software**: Team had to manually stitch outputs from different AI tools
- **Character consistency**: Persistent struggle maintaining identical characters across episodes
- **Dynamic controllability**: Limited control over character movements and physics
- **Lip-sync**: Matching mouth movements to dialogue remained problematic
- **Constant iteration**: "Study latest models while creating" — tech evolved faster than production timeline

### Regulatory Breakthrough

Successfully obtaining the Network Drama Distribution License proved:
- AIGC content CAN enter regulated Chinese broadcast channels
- Full content review + technical quality requirements are achievable with AI
- Established precedent for all future AI drama regulatory filings

# 🏭 2026 INDUSTRY STANDARD SOP (Composite from All Cases)

> This represents the consensus production workflow emerging across all successful AI drama studios in 2026.

## Phase 1: Script & Structuring

| Element | Standard Practice | Recommended Tools |
|:--------|:-----------------|:------------------|
| **Input** | Novel, outline, or creative concept | — |
| **Output** | "Table Script" (表格化剧本) | DeepSeek V4, Doubao (豆包), Claude 3.5 |
| **Format** | Shot #, camera angle (景别), visual description, character action/state, lighting, dialogue, camera movement | — |

## Phase 2: Character Lock & Asset Library

| Element | Standard Practice | Recommended Tools |
|:--------|:-----------------|:------------------|
| **Three-view Generation** | Front, side, half-body reference images per character | Flux Pro, Nano Banana |
| **Identity Anchor (ID)** | Assign unique tag per character forced in all prompts | — |
| **Lightweight Path** | Midjourney "cref" for quick single-character lock | Midjourney |
| **Professional Path** | LoRA fine-tuning: upload 5-10 HD character images, train model | Custom LoRA |
| **Visual Bible** | Project-level style pack: art style, color palette, lighting rules | Huiying AI Visual Bible |

## Phase 3: Storyboard & Video Generation

| Element | Standard Practice | Recommended Tools |
|:--------|:-----------------|:------------------|
| **Key Frame** | Generate one "core base image" as visual tone anchor | — |
| **Shot Generation** | First-frame + last-frame control for transition continuity | Seedance 2.0, Kling 3.0, Vidu |
| **Batch Production** | Generate all shots per episode in batch | Platform Agent systems |
| **Defect Repair** | Inpainting for local facial/body defects | AI inpainting tools |

## Phase 4: Audio & Post-Production

| Element | Standard Practice | Recommended Tools |
|:--------|:-----------------|:------------------|
| **Voice Cloning** | Each character gets dedicated cloned voice | Clone Voice, Jianying AI |
| **BGM/SFX** | AI-generated background music and sound effects | AI music generators |
| **Auto-editing** | Intelligent clip matching: video + dialogue + BGM + transitions | Jianying (剪映) |
| **Quality Enhancement** | AI super-resolution upscaling (1080P+ required) | 蝶变3.0 (Diebian 3.0) |

## Phase 5: QC & Compliance

| Element | Standard Practice |


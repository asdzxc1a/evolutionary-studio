## Channel-Specific Protocols

### Instagram DM (8 letters)
1. **Send from official Zorepad Kazok business account** — must have professional bio, highlights, and portfolio
2. **Time of day:** Tuesday–Wednesday, 10:00–11:30 AM UK time (highest open rates)
3. **Format:** Clean text, no attachments initially. If they respond, follow up with a visual PDF/lookbook
4. **Follow-up protocol:** One gentle follow-up after 10 days if no response. Then silent for 30 days before any second attempt
5. **Bio must include:** Monaco 📍 | Luxury Children's Events | By Invitation ✨

### Physical Letter (2 letters — Federer, Duke of Westminster)
1. **Stationery:** Heavyweight cream or white card stock, letterpress or engraved, with Zorepad Kazok monogram
2. **Envelope:** Hand-addressed, no printed labels
3. **Federer:** Send to TEAM8 management company offices
4. **Duke of Westminster:** Send to Grosvenor Group, 70 Grosvenor Street, London W1K 3JP — marked "Private & Confidential, FAO Personal Assistant to the Duke of Westminster"
5. **Enclosure:** Business card only. No brochures, no promotional material
6. **Follow-up:** None unless they acknowledge receipt

## Critical Do's and Don'ts

### DO ✅
- Reference children by name (verified from public sources)
- Match tone to tier (formal vs. warm)
- Position the gift as "before any business conversation"
- Emphasise Monaco + Côte d'Azur as the core geography
- Frame every offer as exclusive and personalised

### DON'T ❌
- Never reference divorces, separations, or relationship drama
- Never contact Tamara and Petra within the same week
- Never follow up more than once
- Never use the word "free" — use "complimentary" or "our gift"
- Never mention the Prince George connection for the Duke
- Never reference Wagatha Christie for Coleen
- Never reference TOWIE era for Sam
- Never reference Love Island drama for Molly-Mae
